About Anthony Sandoval

Principal & Global Connector

Born in Caracas, Venezuela. British citizen educated at the London School of Economics (Philosophy & Economics). With more than two decades of experience in real estate, finance, and strategic partnerships, Anthony bridges visionary projects with aligned capital.

Mission: To direct capital toward projects that improve communities, preserve natural systems, create financial value, and leave future generations better off.

Global citizen. Musician. Father. Passionate advocate for regenerative development and impact investing at scale.
